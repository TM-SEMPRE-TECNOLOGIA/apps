## Quick orientation
- **Foundations:** `colors_and_type.css` — link or inline.
- **Cards:** `preview/*.html` registered in the Design System tab.
- **UI kit:** `ui_kits/autorelatorio-v2/` — interactive worked example.

- **Type:** Roboto Slab (display) · Inter (UI) · JetBrains Mono (technical).
- **Color north star:** laranja construção `#C8541C`. Sem gradientes. Hairlines, papel, sombras discretas.
